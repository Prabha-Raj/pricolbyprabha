Committed to being

Pricol Limited is one of India’s leading automotive technology and precision engineered solutions company, headquartered in Coimbatore, India. Commencing operations in the year 1975 and being committed to attain leadership and excellence, the company has evolved into a reputed brand in the global automotive industry. By being customer centric and pushing the boundaries on product and process technology and innovation, Pricol, today, is recognized as a preferred partner to many leading automotive Original Equipment Manufacturers across the world.
